How should the guided tour work?

This is the "presentation mode" that walks Van's Auto through the dashboard. Pick the style that feels right.

A

Spotlight + Tooltip Tour

A "Next →" button in the corner starts the tour. Each step dims the rest of the screen and highlights one section with a tooltip bubble explaining what it does. Like a product onboarding tutorial. Feels polished and interactive.

Revenue This Month

"This card shows total revenue collected. You'd see this update live when connected to your POS." → Next

B

Side Panel Narration

A slide-out panel on the right narrates each section as you scroll or click through. The dashboard stays fully visible. The panel acts like a script — "Here's your appointments view. Notice the bay utilization bar…" Great for a screen share presentation.

📍 Step 3 of 8 — Appointments

Your schedule view shows today's bookings and bay availability at a glance. Click any appointment to see the full work order.

C

Both — Auto Tour + Free Explore

A "Start Tour" button triggers the spotlight walkthrough. When the tour ends (or they skip it), the full dashboard is freely explorable. Best of both — structured pitch first, then they can poke around on their own.
