OPTIONAL: FORM SUBMISSION SETUP
================================================================================

The appointment form currently shows a success message but doesn't save data.

To capture form submissions, choose one:

A) FORMSPREE (EASIEST - Free tier: 50 submissions/month)
   1. Sign up at formspree.io
   2. Create new form, copy your Form ID
   3. Find this line in index.html:
      <form id="appointment-form" novalidate>
   4. Change to:
      <form id="appointment-form" action="https://formspree.io/f/YOUR_FORM_ID" method="POST" novalidate>
   5. Submissions now go to your email

   Cost: Free (50/month), $20/month for more
   Setup time: 2 minutes

B) NETLIFY FORMS (If you deploy to Netlify)
   1. Add this to the <form> tag:
      data-netlify="true"
   2. Deploy to Netlify
   3. Submissions appear in Netlify dashboard

   Cost: Free (up to 100 form submissions/month)
   Setup time: 1 minute

C) CUSTOM BACKEND (Advanced)
   Wire the form action to your own email service or database
   See PRODUCTION_CHECKLIST.md for details

   Cost: Varies by service
   Setup time: 15-30 minutes
